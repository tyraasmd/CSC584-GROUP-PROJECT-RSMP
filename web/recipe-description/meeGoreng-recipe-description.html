Mee Goreng (Spicy Fried Noodles)

Category: Dinner

Ingredient - for 2-3 servings:

1. 150 grams yellow noodles (fresh or dried)
2. 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
3. 1 onion (diced)
4. 2 cloves garlic(minced)
5. 2 birds eye chilies, sliced (adjust for spice preference)
6. 100 grams chicken breast, sliced (or other protein like tofu)
7. 100 grams bean sprouts
8. 2 eggs, beaten
9. 2 tablespoons kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)
10. 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
11. 1 tablespoon soy sauce
12. 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
13. Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:


Step 1 : Cook Noodles. If using dried noodles, cook them according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

Step 2 : Heat Oil. Heat oil in a wok or large pan over medium heat.

Step 3 : Saut√© Aromatics. Add onion and cook until softened. Then, add garlic and chilies, stir-frying for another minute until fragrant.

Step 4 : Cook Protein. Add chicken (or other protein) and cook until browned.

Step 5 : Scramble Eggs: Push ingredients to the side and add eggs. Scramble the eggs until cooked through.
Step 6 : Incorporate Noodles. Add cooked noodles and bean sprouts to the pan. Toss to combine.

Step 7 : Seasoning. Pour in kecap manis, oyster sauce, soy sauce, and turmeric. Toss everything together to coat the noodles evenly.

Step 8 : Adjust Seasoning. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 9 : Serve. Serve hot and enjoy!
